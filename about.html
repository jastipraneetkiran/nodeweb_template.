About Us

ZonalDesk is a unique initiative to ensure the quality engineering services in rural area based on the proven service frameworks and processes by utilizing modern technology platform. Process Automation of the system provides transparency and control for the service delivery. Customer QRS is one of the key module of ZonalDesk where customer interactions are captured and processed.

The real issues faced by the rural area is mainly the lack of infrastructure facilities and other resources which is mandatory for the industrial operations. Utilizing the widely used communication media with centralized system and process is what Zonal Desk is targeting at.

If we can provide the quality engineering services in rural area; by engaging dedicated engineers with defined process along with proper connectivity and in affordable price will accelerate the rural development. Soon village become smarter than city; companies will prefer to work in villages than metro cities; with availability of resources and infrastructure resources.
